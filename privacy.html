This Privacy Policy applies to the Classic Bird application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices, developed by Barkat Game Studios (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider") as a free game service.

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect sensitive personal information through the Application. Advertising partners may collect certain technical device data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

The Application may offer optional in-app purchases in future updates, including visual customizations, cosmetic elements, convenience features, or ad-removal enhancements.

All transactions are handled securely through official platform distribution providers such as Google Play.

Data Storage

Gameplay progress, settings, best personal scores, and related game configurations are stored locally on the user's device. Clearing application storage data may reset this information.

Future versions of the Application may also support optional cloud-based saving systems.

Analytics and Diagnostics

The Application may collect anonymous analytics, crash reports, and diagnostic information to help improve application stability, performance, balance, and overall user experience.
